Jumbo Pumpkin Pie Pancake for One

Description

Like I said in the scrambled eggs recipe, I got that dietary restriction rizz. Greek yogurt is a quick and easy snack that keeps my blood sugar happy happy happy. This recipe is so delicious and easy to whip up, even if you don't know the difference between a doonk and a dash. Most of the time, however, I do just mix a little spoon of sugar-free jelly in my Greek yogurt. Come to find out that even natural fruit sugar makes my pancreas sad, so I'll be sticking to stevia from now on.

This recipe is taken from BrianaThomas.com.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup Greek yogurt or cottage cheese (or a combination of the two)
  • 2 T old fashioned oats (use gluten free if necessary)*
  • 2 T chopped fresh cranberries
  • ⅛ tsp. almond extract
  • ⅛ tsp. maple extract
  • 3 doonks THM Pure Stevia Extract Powder (a doonk is 1/32 tsp.)
  • Dash of salt
  • Chopped unsweetened baker's chocolate, 85% dark chocolate, or Lily's brand chocolate

Steps

  1. Stir all the ingredients together and enjoy! Yields one serving.
