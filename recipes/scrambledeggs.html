Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs on a plate

Ingredients

  • Three eggs
  • 1 Tbs of butter (more if you prefer)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put the butter in a nonstick skillet and turn in on. While the butter is heating up, whisk the eggs in a bowl with a few pinches of salt.
  2. When the eggs are whisked, pour them into the pan. Stir constantly with a spatula. The eggs should absorb the butter.
  3. When the eggs are cooked to your liking, turn them out onto a plate. Add pepper if desired and enjoy.
